About Us

About TRUSCREN Ghana

TRUSCREN Ghana is the official distributor of TRUSCREN premium phone screens in Ghana — and home of the TRUSCREN TECH HUB, a professional device repair training school in Accra. We're part of the trusted TRUSCREN brand, a world-leading supplier of premium phone screens serving 38 countries with a defect rate under 0.5%.

We supply genuine, high-quality screens to technicians, repair shops, and wholesalers across Ghana. Through the TECH HUB, we teach practical, job-ready skills in phone screen repair, circuit board soldering, and EV battery diagnostics. Our mission is to make device repair easier and to train the next generation of skilled technicians across Africa.
